WHO

We're just a bunch of queer students at The University of Texas at Austin.

WHAT

Pronouns. Make sure you use people's preferred pronouns when speaking of and/or addressing them. Not sure which pronouns to use? Ask them nicely. Using gender neutral prounouns is always your safest bet, as it makes language generic and inclusive.

WHY

Gender neutrality is important, and one should strive to speak in a way that would not discriminate against or insult people.

HOW

Individuals get to choose their preferred pronouns, and no one else. Making sure that your language is in accordance with their desires will improve understanding, and avoid discrimitation.
